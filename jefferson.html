Thomas Jefferson

I’m Thomas Jefferson, a democratic republican, and I was the third president of the United States. I served from 1801 to 1809, two terms. I was born on April 13, 1743, in Shadwell VA. What were my qualifications? I spent six years roaming the woods as a child and studying books. By nine I was in boarding school with a minister teacher until he was 16. In 1760 I went to college for science, mathematics, rhetoric, philosophy, and literature. I then pursued legal studies and studied the aspects of the law. I worked with 4 other men on the declaration of independence. I served under the Virginia house of delegates, two years as the governor of virginia, I became washington’s secretary of state.I worked on the declaration of independence, served under the Virginia house of delegates, two years as the governor of Virginia, proceeding to become Washington’s secretary of state. As president I purchased the Louisiana territory for quite the bargain of $15 million and abolished slave trade.
